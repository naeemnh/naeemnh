## General Tone Notes

**Do:**

- Be specific over generic
- Show personality without forcing it
- Write like you're talking to a smart friend
- Lead with what makes you interesting

**Don't:**

- Use buzzwords: "passionate," "synergy," "leverage"
- Oversell: "revolutionary," "cutting-edge," "world-class"
- Be self-deprecating to the point of undermining yourself
- Write long paragraphs â€” break them up

**Your voice should feel:**

- Competent but not cocky
- Warm but professional
- Clever but clear
